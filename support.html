Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Tracking

How can I track my shipment?

Enter your tracking number on our homepage or use our mobile app to track your shipment in real-time.

What are your delivery times?

Delivery times vary by service and destination. Express delivery typically takes 1-3 business days, while standard shipping takes 3-7 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to over 200 countries. Rates and delivery times vary by destination.

Billing & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods including PayPal and digital wallets.

How can I get an invoice for my shipment?

Invoices are automatically generated and sent to your registered email address. You can also download them from your account dashboard.

Claims & Returns

What should I do if my package is damaged?

Document the damage with photos and contact our support team immediately. File a claim within 24 hours of delivery for fastest processing.

Is shipping insurance included?

Basic coverage is included for all shipments. Additional insurance can be purchased for valuable items.
